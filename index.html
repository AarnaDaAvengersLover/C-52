Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they ise to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Pandas

  1. Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  2. An adult panda can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!
  3. Pandas have carnivorous teeth, but they eat bamboo and fruit.

Interesting Facts about Teddys

  1. A teddy has been into space! Magellan T Bear boarded Space Shuttle Discovery in 1995 as part of a school project.
  2. The correct term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  3. They aren’t just for children – 40 per cent of teddy sales are for adults

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  1. Their short adult life is not unusual for bugs.
  2. Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  3. Most have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes.

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!
  3. When a camel finally does find water, he can drink up to 40 gallons in one go.
